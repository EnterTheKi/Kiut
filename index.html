Coffees

Strong Coffees

Espresso
A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's the base for many other coffee drinks.
Taste: Rich and intense with a bold flavor.

Ristretto
A shorter, more concentrated shot of espresso, made with the same amount of coffee but half the amount of water.
Taste: Stronger and more intense than a regular espresso, with a richer flavor.

Normal Coffees

Latte
Made with one shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam.
Taste: Creamy and smooth, with a mild coffee flavor.

Cappuccino
Similar to a latte but with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.
Taste: Balanced and frothy, with a stronger coffee flavor than a latte.
